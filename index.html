Bilkent University

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Bilkent University, working in quantum information theory in a project lead by Cihan Okay. I received my doctorate in Mathematics from Bilkent in January 2023, advised by Ozgun Unlu.

My research interests lie in the interaction between category theory and algebraic topology. More specifically, I am interested in homotopy coherent categories and operads. I also have an interest in categorical quantum theory. Check out my writings and talks pages for more details.

I have taught Math courses at Bilkent University (TR), University of Utah (USA) and Metropolitan University (ALB). See my teaching page for further information.
